App windows (Cage): run one Linux GUI app in a floating window or fullscreen, no full desktop needed. New: a searchable list of your installed apps with real icons; per-app and global resolution + scale so fonts aren't tiny; and a reworked fullscreen that fills the screen, clears rounded corners, refits on rotation, and resizes live with a three-finger pinch (a new "?" in the fullscreen menu explains the gestures). Default-display settings now collapse into an expandable row.

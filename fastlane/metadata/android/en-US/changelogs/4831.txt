- Terminal: the keyboard toolbar's edit mode now separates the fixed controls (done, desktop, add-key, settings) from the draggable keys, and a new "Edit-mode controls" setting (Settings → Keyboard toolbar) lets you group them Split (both ends, default), Left, or Right — so you can put all the immovable buttons on one side, away from the keys you rearrange (#224).
- Agent/MCP: new send_to_agent tool — delivers a message to another agent's REPL (or any raw-mode prompt) as one cleanly submitted turn (bracketed paste, a settle, then Enter) and returns the resulting screen, without hand-assembling the keystrokes (#19).

🎨 **Windows RDP: colour is back** — connecting to a Windows desktop over RDP had turned the whole screen into a flat embossed grey, wallpaper and all. The RemoteFX-Progressive decoder was dropping the low-frequency part of every tile in the "extrapolate" mode Windows 8/10/11 always use, so flat areas collapsed to grey and only edges survived. The decoder now rebuilds that band and colour returns. (#418, thanks ZGLinus)

🧪 **Windows RDP: experimental fix for the black squares (opt-in)** — the v5.81.5 colour fix left scattered black squares on some Windows RDP desktops, because Haven decoded the first pass of each RemoteFX-Progressive tile but not the later "upgrade" refinement passes. This adds that decoder, but it's off by default and experimental (not yet verified against real Windows). To try it: Settings → Diagnostics → "RDP: decode progressive upgrade tiles". If the picture looks worse, turn it off. (#418, thanks ZGLinus)

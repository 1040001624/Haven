USB/IP device forwarding now works for serial devices, not just FIDO keys. It used to export only the first USB interface (built for a YubiKey), so a USB-serial board — a multi-interface CDC device like an ESP32 / LilyGo T-Watch — lost its data interface and no serial port appeared on the host. Now non-FIDO devices export every interface: a phone-attached board shows up as /dev/ttyACM* on the remote host, so you can flash it with esptool / PlatformIO over the bridge.

SFTP and SMB media now stream instead of downloading first. Previewing or converting a file on an SMB share — and converting over SFTP — reads through the same Range-capable loopback bridge that rclone and SFTP preview already use, so ffmpeg pulls just the header instead of the whole file (a large SMB clip now previews quickly). Agent-driven APK installs over a slow backend also report progress instead of a false timeout, and surface their final result in get_app_info.

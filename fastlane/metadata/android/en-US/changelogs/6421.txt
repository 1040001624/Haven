• F-Droid builds work again. Every F-Droid build since 5.68.41 failed before compiling anything, so none of the releases after it reached F-Droid users: the cross-compiled Wayland desktop stack needs a wayland-scanner matching our vendored Wayland exactly, and F-Droid's build image moved to a newer one. Haven now builds that tool from its own source, so the image's version no longer matters. No change to the app itself.

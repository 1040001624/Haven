The agent-approval notification now has Allow and Deny buttons. When an AI agent (over Haven's MCP endpoint) tries an action that needs approval while Haven is in the background, tap Allow (after unlocking your device) to let the agent's retry through, or Deny to dismiss — no need to open the app. The action still never runs without your explicit OK, and approving is scoped to that tool for a couple of minutes.

Also: your phone's shared storage is now mounted at /sdcard inside the Linux (proot) terminal, not just /storage/emulated/0 — grant Haven storage access and `ls /sdcard` lists your files.

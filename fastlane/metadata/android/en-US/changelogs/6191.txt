🔌 Mosh sessions now survive network outages of any length. The client no longer gives up after 8 seconds of silence — it detects the server's explicit shutdown announcement instead, keeps retrying with the same key until connectivity returns, and rebinds its socket periodically so IP changes (Wi-Fi → mobile) recover automatically. The disconnect countdown banner is now a "No server contact for Ns — retrying" indicator. Thanks to Biotoza for the report and groundwork (#365).
